COLLEGE & HOSTEL

THE COLLEGE

This is my beautiful college . I joined here in 2019. It is one of the oldest colleges in India though it looks like a fresh art.... I have a lot of good memories connected with this. Met new friends, Came out of my state for the first time, My first hostel life and many more.

THE CANTEEN

This is my college canteen. Nothing much but, I like its remodelled version you see here. I am here only at the times of fests.

HOSTEL-B

Here comes the Hostel B, Chitrakot. My First hostel ever....I enjoyed a year in this hostel. It was immense joy being a hostelite during my engineering. Late night parties, Spending hours in the mess, skipping Breakfasts and many more.

THE HOSTEL

This was the actual view of my hostel. Actually we do have few more hostels for the first year students.... Hostel A,B,D,E,F,G (E,G for girls). We are randomly alloted to these hostels. Only few students get hostels based on their JEE rank. From 2nd year we have a common hostel till 4th year.

MY ROOM

I was alloted with room no.47. One more roomie shared the stay. We generally have 2-3 persons per room.

THE CORRIDOR

This is the view of the corridors in our hostel. Hostels are also rich in greenary.

THE NORMAL MEAL

NIT Raipur hostels meals generally consists of roti/poori, rice, dal/sambar, curry. On wednesdays we have Paneer/egg.

THE SUNDAY MEAL

I was not fond of North Indian food at all but, I am fond of this sunday meal in my hostel....we have a salad, papad, Chicken/paneer, Jeera rice, poori and custard.
